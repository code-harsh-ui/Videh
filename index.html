WELCOME TO VEDEH

The Word Videh literally means beyond the body, both physically and mentally. Videh is the name given to a great king of Mithila, Raja Janaka. Janaka is revered for his non-attachment to material possessions, despite being a great king of his time. He was the first enlightened king (Rajarishi).

Our training programs are crafted and delivered by a group of Certified Isha Hatha Yoga Teachers, meticulously trained under the guidance of Sadhguru. Immerse yourself in the purest form of yoga and meditations directly derived from the source, Adi Yogi (The Shiva), with our carefully curated programs. Experience the transformative power of our training, designed to help you engineer your inner being and manifest outer well-being. Join us on this journey of self-discovery and holistic growth.

Upa yoga

Upa is a simple yet powerful set of 10 practices that activate the joints, muscles and energy system, bringing ease to the whole system. Based on a sophisticated understanding of the body’s mechanics, Upa Yoga dispels inertia in the body’s energy and brings ease to the whole system.

Benefits

  • Relieves physical stress and tiredness
  • Strengthens the joints and muscles
  • Rejuvenates the body after periods of inactivity
  • Negates the effects of jetlag and long travel
